## Executive Summary

Lever's revenue model follows a **freemium SaaS approach** optimized for mobile app stores. Launch free to maximize adoption, then convert users to premium through value-driven features.

**Target Metrics:**
- Free tier: 10,000+ DAU within 90 days
- Premium conversion: 3-5% (industry standard for productivity apps)
- Average Revenue Per User (ARPU): $24-48/year
- Break-even: ~500 premium subscribers

---

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Premium Subscription Tier ($4.99/month or $39.99/year)

**Core Value Props:**
- **Advanced Analytics:** Detailed financial tracking, goal progress charts, ROI calculations
- **Custom Planning:** Personalized faith/family/finance action plans
- **Priority Support:** Direct access to Joshua's frameworks and methodologies
- **Export/Sync:** Data export, cross-device sync, integrations

**Why This Price:**
- Comparable to other personal development apps (Headspace $5/mo, Calm $6/mo)
- Below productivity tools (Notion $8/mo, Todoist $5/mo) but above simple utilities
- Annual discount drives retention (67% savings = strong incentive)

### 2. One-Time Premium Features

**Lever Pro Unlock:** $19.99 one-time
- All premium features permanently unlocked
- Appeals to users who prefer ownership over subscriptions
- Higher customer lifetime value for engaged users

### 3. Future Revenue Opportunities (Post-Launch)

**Content Partnerships:** $2-5/month add-ons
- Exclusive Joshua Levy content (daily insights, video teachings)
- Guest expert content (financial advisors, marriage counselors, pastors)

**Coaching Tier:** $49-99/month (limited spots)
- 1:1 monthly check-ins with Joshua or certified coaches
- Personalized action plans and accountability

## Launch Strategy (Revenue-Focused)

### Phase 1: Free Launch (Months 1-3)
**Goal:** Maximize adoption, validate product-market fit
- No premium features visible (but code ready)
- Focus on organic growth and user feedback
- Track key engagement metrics (session length, retention, feature usage)

### Phase 2: Premium Rollout (Months 4-6)
**Goal:** Convert engaged users to premium
- Soft launch premium features to beta users
- A/B test pricing ($4.99 vs $6.99 monthly)
- Implement in-app purchase flows and subscription management
- Launch referral program (1 month free for successful referrals)

### Phase 3: Growth Acceleration (Months 7-12)
**Goal:** Scale to profitable growth
- App Store optimization and paid acquisition
- Content marketing through Joshua's channels
- Partnership opportunities with complementary apps/services
- Introduce coaching tier for high-value users

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

### Primary Metrics
- **Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR):** Target $5,000 by month 12
- **Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC):** <$15 for organic, <$25 for paid
- **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV):** $120+ (24 months avg retention)
- **Premium Conversion Rate:** 3-5% of DAU

### Secondary Metrics
- **App Store Rating:** Maintain >4.5 stars
- **Daily Active Users (DAU):** 10,000+ by month 12
- **Session Length:** >5 minutes average
- **Feature Adoption:** 80% of users engage with core features weekly

## Risk Mitigation

### Revenue Risks
- **Low Conversion:** If <2% convert, consider lower price point or different value props
- **High Churn:** If >8% monthly churn, improve onboarding and engagement features
- **App Store Rejection:** Have web app version ready as backup distribution

### Competitive Risks
- **Big Player Entry:** Focus on niche advantage (faith integration) and personal brand
- **Pricing Pressure:** Differentiate through premium experience, not just features
